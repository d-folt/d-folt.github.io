5 seond rule.

I've been dealing with procrastination lately. bitter truth is I enjoy procrastinating but it also makes you feel guilty. Knowing that you need to do work but instead you choose to chill around. I came across a method called 5 second rule and what it does basically is limiting your thoughts and let the action carry on, without thinking consequences. I've been putting this into practice and had some real results. You can try it too.
